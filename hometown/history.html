CITY OF SMILE


history


Bacolod was founded in 1770 and was named after the Ilonggo dialect term "Bakolod," which means "Stonehill" Is presently one of the Philippines' most progressive and prosperous cities.

Geography


The city has a square area of 162.67 and is located on the northwest coast of the island of Negros. It had a population of around 187,300 people in 1970. It has a pleasantly cool environment during the rainy season and a tropical and humid atmosphere ideal for a summer holiday. Hiligaynon or Ilonggo is the main dialect, which is intermingled with Bisaya dialect that is spoken by the minority.

Along with being hospitable and kind, Bacolod Local residents are renowned for their sweet demeanor and indescribably melodious voice and accent when conversing. They are known as the sweetest and loving people in the Philippines. Bacolod City is a highly recommended place to visit in the Philippines especially during the Masskara festival, with its rich culture and amazing people you will surely leave this place with a smile.
