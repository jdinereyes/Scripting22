SUGAR BOWL OF THE PHILIPPINES

Bacolod City

The “Sugar Bowl of the Philippines” or the "City of Smiles" titles and the dazzling and flamboyant Masskara Festival are both used to describe the Negros Occidental's capital, popularly known as Bacolod City. It is considered to have the perfect harmony of urban development and rural beauty as it offers artistic culture, culinary, and recreational activities. It also has a hospitable community, gorgeous scenery, and a rich history.
